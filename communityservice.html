I also have extensive volunteering experience. For example, I have acted as a helper volunteer for my local chess center during their summer camps. In the summer before 9th grade, I assisted in a care center in China for disabilities. I made instructive posters, taught basic English lessons, and played games with the patients. During my freshman year, I helped out at a local Chinese school every Saturday morning by supervising students, printing copies for teachers, and selling loaves of bread during festivals or celebrations. Currently, I have been volunteering at the Frist Art Museum two to three times a month for the past school year, where duties include washing art materials, guiding participants through various art activities, organizing materials during closing time, and taking out the trash. I am also a member of my school's Mu Alpha Theta organization, earning tutoring hours by engaging in after-school math labs once a week. In my junior year, I was a library aide for my school, helping students on their math homework or organizing books and computers.
