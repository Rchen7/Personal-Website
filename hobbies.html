One of my favorite hobbies is tennis. I like this as a sport because it relies more on technique rather than just raw strength. I enjoy being a baseline player in doubles and also practicing my serve in general. In addition to the playing and exercise itself, tennis also gives me a way to get adequate sunlight each day and make friendships with my teammates. I have been playing this sport since I was around 7 years old, and I have been a member of my school's varsity tennis team for the past three years.

Another one of my hobbies is cello. I have been playing this instrument for 10 years now, since I was 7 1/2 years old. Some of my favorite parts about this activity is getting to know the people sitting around me in various orchestras as well as finally getting the hang of a passage that I struggled for a while on. I have participated in various symphonies at my school or in city-wide events, such as in the Mid-state Orchestra or the Vanderbilt Youth Orchestras (Repertory, Philharmonia, and Curb).

Finally, as an enjoyer of strategy, I have also been an avid fan of chess. I actually encountered this activity by accident after rummaging in the board games closet in preschool. Since then, I have played in, qualified for, and won numerous tournaments; for example, I have played in the Nashville City Championship many times. My highest USCF rating has been 1568. Recently, my favorite thing about this game is doing the tactics and puzzles; I am learning to develop an intuition for knowing when the move I've found is the right one.
